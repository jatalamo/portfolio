Rutgers Full-Stack Web Development Student

Thanks for your interest in learning about me! I am excited to share a bit about my life and my journey as a Rutger's Web Development Student at the age of 50!

I am New Jersey native and have lived here most of my life, (except for 5 years where I lived in Mississippi & New Orleans.) I am married to Joe, have 2 children, Joey and Gigi and we live in Hillsborough, New Jersey. We have 3 rescued dogs and I am a very active animal rescue volunteer. I love to travel and bring my kids to unique places each year. "Never the same vacation twice until you've seen it all" is my motto! For fun, I am a novice author of a blog entitled "Things I saw Today" where I simply "Stop & Smell the Roses of Daily life." You'll pretty much be walking in my shoes! I hope you will check it out!

My first career was in the Hotel/Casino Industry, lasting 15 years and spanning three states. I was a Blackjack and Baccarat Dealer, Supervisor, Trainer and finally, a Manager. It was alot of fun, but I needed some creativity! With the help and advice of several family members in the Programming field, In 1998 I decided on a career change into Computer Programming. I attended The Chubb Institute of Computer Programming for their one-year training course where I achieved the status of "Honor Student." I learned COBOL, JCL, Visual Basic and some other now outdated stuff! I knew I found my passion! Upon graduation, I was employed by Prudential Financial as a PeopleSoft Analyst, I was also newly married! The following year, we welcomed the arrival of our first child. Faced with a really touch choice, I decided to stay home, raise kids and forego my new career. I felt lucky to even have this option, as so many moms don't!...And time slipped away, until now, 15 years later!

I never forgot about programming and the sense of accomplishment it gave me and knew I would return to it "one day". Well, "one day" is Now! I am thrilled to be a student again, learning the newest technologies of Web development at Rutger's Coding Bootcamp.

Students in this program will learn a full-stack, including:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • FTP
  • JavaScript
  • jQuery
  • Node.js
  • Database Theory
  • ActiveRecord
  • Command Line
  • Git
  • Collaborative Software Development

With nothing to stop me now, I truly am looking forward to getting back out into the professional world and contributing my creative passion and talent to the universe. I'm excited! Programming, I have missed you!
